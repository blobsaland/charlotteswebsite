About

Everything must be beautiful. Not only does the work of Charlotte stimulate your eyes, but your ears are also filled with pleasure. Your mind and body will start moving and grooving.

Charlotte (she/her) is a visual artist and DJ based in Rotterdam. Colorful organic shapes and lines change into a dynamic and dreamy image. Decorative and playful as opposed to political and poetic. People/ characters, colors, clothes, patterns, words/ poems, activism, disco aesthetics, art nouveau, and her own imagination are essential sources of inspiration which is very present in her work.
